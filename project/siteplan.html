Overview

Site Name

Ghana Treasures

This name highlights the rich cultural, historical, and natural “treasures” found throughout Ghana. It's memorable and reflects the purpose of showcasing the country’s top tourist destinations.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate and inspire travelers and locals about Ghana’s most popular and culturally significant tourist destinations. It will feature profiles of top historical landmarks, natural parks, beaches, and cultural heritage sites in Ghana, as well as tips for travelers.

Scenarios

  • What are the top five must-see historical sites in Ghana?

  • Are there nature reserves or parks in Ghana where I can go on safari?
