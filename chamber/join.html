NP Membership Details

Our NP (Non-Profit) Membership is designed specifically for non-profit organizations to help them connect with the business community.

Benefits:

  • Basic listing in our online business directory
  • Monthly newsletter with updates and opportunities
  • Access to networking events and mixers
  • Participation in community service initiatives

Cost: Free

To qualify for NP Membership, your organization must be a registered non-profit with valid documentation.

Bronze Membership Details

Our Bronze Membership is perfect for small businesses looking to establish connections and grow their network.

Benefits:

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Enhanced directory listing with logo and description
  • 10% discount on all chamber events and workshops
  • Access to business training workshops
  • Monthly business tips and resources

Cost: GHS 500 per year

Silver Membership Details

Our Silver Membership offers increased visibility and benefits for growing businesses.

Benefits:

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Featured spot in our monthly newsletter
  • 25% discount on all chamber events
  • Priority customer support
  • Advertising opportunities in chamber publications
  • Invitation to exclusive roundtable discussions

Cost: GHS 1,000 per year

Gold Membership Details

Our Gold Membership provides the highest level of benefits and visibility for established businesses.

Benefits:

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Spotlight feature on our homepage (rotating basis)
  • 50% discount on all chamber events
  • VIP access to premium events and conferences
  • Dedicated account manager
  • Exclusive networking with top business leaders
  • Complimentary tickets to annual gala

Cost: GHS 2,000 per year
